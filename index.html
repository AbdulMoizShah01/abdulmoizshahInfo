About Me.

Hi, I’m Abdul Moiz Shah, a passionate Software Engineering student at PAF-IAST, with a deep interest in Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, and full-stack development. I love building smart systems that bridge the gap between real-world problems and tech-driven solutions. Over the past few years, I’ve worked on diverse projects—from developing a Pashto speech-to-text system using Whisper ASR to crafting cross-platform mobile apps in Flutter and building dynamic web applications with React.js. My journey in tech has been driven by curiosity, creativity, and a constant desire to improve and learn.

I enjoy taking complex ideas and turning them into simple, elegant, and user-friendly experiences. Whether it's training machine learning models, developing intuitive UIs, or optimizing code for performance, I’m always up for the challenge. When I’m not coding, you’ll likely find me exploring new tech trends, working on side projects, or helping others in the developer community.
